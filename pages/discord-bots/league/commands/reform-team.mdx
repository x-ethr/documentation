# `/reform-team` command

<Callout type={"info"}>It is recommended to only have Organizers or Moderators use this command</Callout>
<Callout type={"warning"}>
    This command will only work if you did not delete the archived `(Disbanded)` team's role.
</Callout>

Reforms a team that was previously disbanded.

**Required Options**
- `team` *(Role)* - The role of the team to reform
- `captain` *(User)* - The captain of the team

**Additional Options**
- `color` *(Region)* *Optional* - The team color in hexcode
