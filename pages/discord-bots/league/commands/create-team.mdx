# `/create-team` command

Creates a team.

<Callout type={"info"}>It is recommended to only have Organizers or Moderators use this command</Callout>
<Callout type={"warning"}>
    Discord servers are limited to 250 roles. If you need space, delete `(Disbanded)` roles that are archived.
</Callout>

**Required Options**
- `team_name` *(String)* - The name of the team
- `captain` *(User)* - The captain of the team
- `region` *(Region)* - The region of the team

**Additional Options**
- `color` *(String)* *Optional* - The team color in hexcode
